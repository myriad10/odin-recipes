Banana Milkshake

Description

A classic, creamy, and refreshing banana milkshake is perfect for a quick treat or a nutritious breakfast. This simple recipe highlights the natural sweetness of ripe bananas and pairs it with the smoothness of milk for a drink that’s both satisfying and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas (for optimal sweetness and flavor)
  • 2 cups of cold milk (dairy or non-dairy alternatives like almond, oat, or soy milk)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of honey or sugar (optional, depending on how sweet you like it)
  • ½ teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional for added flavor)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for a chilled and frothy texture)

Steps

  1. Peel and slice the bananas into smaller chunks for easier blending.
  2. Place the banana slices in a blender, followed by the cold milk.
  3. Add honey or sugar if desired, and include the vanilla extract for extra flavor.
  4. If you like your milkshake chilled, throw in a few ice cubes.
  5. Blend on high speed until the mixture is smooth and frothy, which should take about 30-45 seconds.
  6. Pour the milkshake into tall glasses and serve immediately.
